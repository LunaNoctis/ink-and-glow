Hello, I'm the person behind Ink & Glow

It started with a single Leuchtturm1917 journal and a pack of Mildliners. What followed was years of hunting down the best pens, the smoothest papers, the most satisfying planners — and sharing every discovery with whoever would listen.

Ink & Glow exists because beautiful stationery is more than just paper and ink. It's the ritual of sitting down to write. The quiet pleasure of a pen that glides just right. The joy of opening a fresh notebook and knowing anything is possible.

Every product featured here has been personally researched, cross-referenced with real reviews, and chosen because it genuinely earns a place on your desk.

What We Believe

Good stationery doesn't have to be expensive — but it does have to be right. We believe in recommending only what we'd actually use ourselves.

We are transparent about how this site works: when you purchase through our Amazon links, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. Full details are on our Affiliate Disclosure page.
